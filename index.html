About Me

Machine Learning Researcher | ADAS Software Engineer | Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering

Passionate about advancing autonomous systems. At Canoo, my role as an ADAS Software Engineer involves delivering advanced control systems and sensor fusion techniques to enhance vehicle safety. In this role, I have strengthened my programming skills in C++ and Python and gained hands-on experience in software development within the automotive industry. Previously, as a Machine Learning Researcher in Robotics at Cornell University, I developed a multi-modal pedestrian trajectory prediction model and a perception-feedback UAV system for dynamic human-robot interactions. These projects, which resulted in multiple papers, have enhanced my expertise in perception, probabilistic reasoning, and human-robot interaction.
